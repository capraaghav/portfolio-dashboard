The privacy promise

Runs locally. Your holdings never leave your machine.

The dashboard reads your broker export, computes everything on your own computer, and stores it under a local ./data/ folder. The only outbound network calls are to Yahoo Finance for live prices and fundamentals — your positions, costs, and account names are never uploaded anywhere.

  • No portfolio data sent to any server
  • Outbound calls only to Yahoo Finance, for prices
  • Delete the local data/ folder to reset completely
  • No account required to run it on your own machine

Performance you can trust

Snapshots · XIRR · benchmark

Daily snapshots build a value timeline over time. XIRR computes the moment you add purchase dates, and a benchmark backtest replays your current basket against NIFTY 50, SENSEX, Bank Nifty, or Midcap — honestly labelled for what it is.

Tax, risk & income

LTCG/STCG · concentration · dividends

A tax view splits LTCG and STCG (using Zerodha's long-term quantity column) with estimated tax and harvesting candidates. A risk view surfaces concentration, top-5 weight, effective holdings, portfolio beta, and sector exposure. Dividends report TTM income and yield per stock.

Tax figures are estimates, surfaced plainly — never dressed up as advice.

Watchlist & rebalancing

Track · plan

Keep a watchlist of stocks you don't own yet — live price, day change, analyst target, saved locally. Set target weights and the rebalance view shows drift and the exact ₹ to buy or sell per holding. It only ever suggests — it never places an order or moves money.
